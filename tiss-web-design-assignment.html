Class Assignment: Plan, Design and Create a Website for You Film

Summary

You have already begun by making a "microsite" for your movie. Your final assignment for this course is going to make a full website for the film and a related cause.

To do this you will be expected to deliver a full set of planning materials as well as the finished site. Your site should align with and support an initiative, organization or cause related to the theme of your film. For example, if your film was about the lives of workers who are now unemployed following the closure of the mills, you could pick 1 or 2 organizations that serve unemployed mill workers and include some support for their work throughout the site.

Deliverables

Your project will be expected to contain the following:

Background Documentation

  • Project Goals

  • Personas

  • User Stories

Research

  • 2-3 Inspiration Sites, with explanation

  • 2-3 competition sites, dealing with a similar area or goal

User Experience Planning

  • Key Workflows: list of 2-3 important and typical user pathways

  • Information Architecture (IA)

User Interface Planning

  • Scribble

  • Wireframes

  • Styleguide: including color palette and font selection, with explanations

Technical Delivery

  • Site code, including all HTML, CSS and images.

  • README.txt
